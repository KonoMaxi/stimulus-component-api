Welcome to the Stimulus-Component-API Proof-of-Concept

Source: https://github.com/KonoMaxi/stimulus-component-api

By clicking on one of the "mount" buttons, a component of the matching framework is mounted on a stimulus-target via a stimulus-action. See src/demo/controllers/ for details

By clicking a "unmount" button, the corresponding component is unmounted out of existence.

Each language's component implements (at least) a counter and a text-message. You can increase the counter by clicking "Stimulus +1" (triggers a stimulus action) or "*Framework* +1" (the component increases the counter) Please note: The counter and the text-message are synchronized with the stimulus-value-api! Thus unmounting and remounting it will yield consisten results.

As its often simpler (clearer structured) to let the stimulus controller handle e.g. form submitting, the controller listens for "action"-events. These will be mapped to a stimulus-actions and allow passing of parameters.
